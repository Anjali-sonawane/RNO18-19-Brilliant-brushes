WHAT IS ART ?

Art is a highly diverse range of human activities engaged in creating visual, auditory,
or performed artifacts— artworks—that express the author’s imaginative or technical skill,
and are intended to be appreciated for their beauty or emotional power.
The oldest documented forms of art are visual arts,
which include images or objects in fields like painting, sculpture, printmaking , photography, and other visual media .
Architecture is often included as one of the visual arts; however, like the decorative arts,
it involves the creation of objects where the practical considerations of use are essential,
in a way that they usually are not in another visual art, like a painting.
